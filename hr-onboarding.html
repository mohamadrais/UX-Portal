HR Onboarding Application

HR onboarding is a part of one of the 2017 priorities "Digitalization". The scope is to provide the new employees who have just signed the offer letter with information about IT Services as well as the about the location where they are joining (Cyberjaya, Prague), it will guide them along different stages they need to go through the first day as well as the 3 months induction program.

User Experience in Action

HR Onboarding App Wireframes

  1. User Research

    As this was a new app there were many suggestion for many features to be a part of that. We had a view from the management level but not from the users perspective, that is when we decided to have a user research (questioning people who have recently joined DHL). Interviewing them led us to 3 different types of personas.

    People new to Malaysia and DHL These are the employees who were not living in Malaysia, but got the job over and joined here. These folks faced quite a problem in many aspects like benchmark salary, living, rent, accomodation, children studies and they did a lot of research for that. Even after coming to Malaysia they still face difficulties like food, groceries and many other stuff.

    People old to Malaysia but new to DHL These guys had almost no issues. But still if new to cyberjaya they wanted to have an overview of the location like accomodation, restaurants, shopping, entertainment.

    The Locals Obviously its their own backyard, they had no issues.

  2. Information Architecture Now we had a huge set of data that we could work on. Categorizing them in terms of primary, secondary and teritary features, following that we performed a card sort categorizing on the features like grouping like items together under each main category that would later become main content tabs.
  3. Wireframing and Interactive Prototype After grouping all the features in similar tabs we now have some what clear structure of what we want to make. Then we designed it in terms of wireframes. Right now its still in progress.
