Our Design Strategy

UX Studio develops the design strategy which promotes the vision of the company from UX perspective based on the three bottom lines - Employee of Choice, Provider of Choice and Investment of Choice. Our design strategy fits into the organization culture to develop and adopt UX standards to improve interaction design of our products and services. Download Maturity Assessment

Our Roadmap

UX Studio presents the roadmap which is derived from the maturity assessment of the organization from the areas of strategy, staffing, process & infrastructure and knowledge & training. Our roadmap strives to shape the organization by promoting UX design adoption, building and up-skilling capabilities, establishing process and methodology in growing towards the Matured UX Practice. Our Principles

UX Studio shares the principles which serves as a foundation in building teams with right mindset, knowledge and belief in practicing the user centered design and methodology. Our guiding principles encourage the team to actively engage in providing creative solutions, continuously learn and deliver products and services which drives user experience to the next level. Welcoming Our Experiences Are Friendly And Inviting. We Keep Them Straightforward So Our Users Can Easily Get Started. Confident Our Experiences Guide Our Users In Moving Through Information To Complete Their Goals. We Create Intuitive Navigation Through Clear Metaphors And Actionable Cues. Valuable Our Experiences Generate Value For Our Users. We Make Our Users Smarter And More Productive By Giving Them The Means To Discover New Insights And Efficiencies. Delightful Our Experiences Thrill Our Users By Exceeding Their Expectations. We Innovate For Our Users To Live Our Brand And Differentiate DHL Download Guiding Principles
