Evaluate – Measuring the Experience

Sit back and relax, this is now the moment of truth. In this stage, you no longer make predictions, you find out.

Measuring UX Impacts and Ongoing Value

Monitor Usability Measures

After launching the product in production, analyze the data and extract meaningful trends through web analytics and measure the user efficiency through task completion. Look out for the improvements observed on the usability metrics during build stage and keep track of these measurements in production.

Implement Surveys

You may use the same set of questionnaires used earlier for benchmarking to capture user satisfaction or conduct a new survey with net promoter score in measuring the user experience.

Project of ROI

Based on the data gathered, populates them into the ROI calculator and showcase your projects to the stakeholders!
